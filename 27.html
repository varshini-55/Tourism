Chaparai Waterfalls



Chaparai Waterfalls also known as Dumbriguda waterfalls, is one of the beautiful scenic spot in Araku. Chaparai Waterfalls Near araku is just about 15 to 20 minutes of journey from Araku Town and is about 13 Km. There will be a Entry Free (Entry Ticket) of Rs. 10/- for each person. There is a lot of food selling point like bamboo chicken, chicken kebabs, bamboo chicken Biryani, bhutta (Corn), tea, sugarcane, snacks, cooldrikns e.t.c.,


The main attraction for tourist for this Chaparai Waterfalls is famous selling bamboo chicken. Most of the Visitors will try to taste it for its spicy taste with the add green chilies. Chaparai Waterfalls is Quite enjoyable place can visit with our families and friends.
